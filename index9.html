Why?

VirtuZoo wants to provide a enjoyable and immersive experience of Africa's biodiverse ecosystem, not only to bring enjoyment to all those that are interested, but bring awareness to the struggles these environments deal with everyday. In spreading the word, we believe that are able inspire, interact, and impact the world with the beauty that is contained in Africa.
