Understanding the World

When looking at Earth as we know it, there is much not known about the continent of Africa. Its contents are filled with a variety of animals, each compelling and unique. They all bring purpose to the world's ecosystem. You are about to embark on a virtual experience full of information about the life found within Africa.

This journey helps one become more aware of how beautiful Africa's biodiverse system is. Since nature is placed on a massive scale, it can be difficult to hone in on the smaller pieces of what make this whole environment thrive. And sometimes, the smallest changes in the food chain come from the biggest animals.
